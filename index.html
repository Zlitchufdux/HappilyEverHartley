Our Story

2011

Huntington Middle School

They met in middle school. Trey remembers snowball dancing with Gabby, but she does not remember their dance. Fast forward to Gabby's 8th grade year and Trey's 7th grade year, Gabby made the first move and commented on his Instagram photo calling him cute. Soon after, they started dating (if you can call it dating in middle school). Eventually, they split when Gabby went to high school. Luckily, their mutual friend Tanner had a get-together at his house one summer evening. Gabby and Trey got chosen by the bottle to kiss. Every since that summer night, they have been together.

May 26th, 2023

The Proposal

Trey took Gabby to Cascade Locks where they went and had dinner at a brewery. Afterwards, they went for a walk to look out at the Columbia River. They went to the top of the hill overlooking the river. Trey got down on one knee and asked Gabby to marry him right there.

Engagement

The day she said yes

Gabby had no idea Trey was going to propose until the week of when he said something out of the ordinary to go get her nails done. Then, she began to get suspicious. Trey was so excited to take Gabby to this cool new brewery in the gorge. She was nervous and excited all at the same time. She kept telling herself that if it doesn't happen tonight, it will be ok. She tried to not get too overly excited incase it did not happen. But then, he got down on one knee! She knew from the very beginning of their relationship that he was going to be her forever person. And here they are about to start their forever together.
